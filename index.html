Evolution of Cinema: Genre Distribution Over Time

Over the span of four decades, from 1980 to 2020, the film industry contributed a remarkable 7,478 movies. The peak of cinematic output, where 200 films per year graced the screens, was observed during the years 2001 to 2005, 2007, 2009, 2013, 2014, and 2016 to 2018.

In a remarkable crescendo, the Comedy genre reigned supreme in 1994, yielding a total of 81 movies. Over this period, there has been a notable trend of increasing movie production, peaking for about 32 years, followed by a sudden decline.
